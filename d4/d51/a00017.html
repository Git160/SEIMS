int bmps::BMPFactory::bmpType ( )

Get BMP type 1 - reach BMPs which are attached to specific reaches and will change the character of the reach. 2 - areal structural BMPs which are corresponding to a specific structure in the watershed and will change the character of subbasins/cells. 3 - areal non-structure BMPs which are NOT corresponding to a specific structure in the watershed and will change the character of subbasins/cells. 4 - point structural BMPs
